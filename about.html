About Virgé Bistro

Established 2024

At Virgé Bistro, we believe every meal tells a story. Established in 2024, our journey began with a simple dream: to bring people together through good food and warm service.

From our sizzling appetizers to our decadent desserts, every dish is thoughtfully prepared using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. We blend tradition and creativity, offering a menu that comforts and excites in equal measure.

Whether you're craving a rich biryani, a refreshing smoothie, or just a cozy corner to share a laugh — Virgé Bistro is your spot. It’s not just food, it’s a feeling.

Where flavor meets passion. 🍽️

Bon Appétit! — From the Virgé Bistro family to yours.
